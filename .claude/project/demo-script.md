
# ReliefRoute Demo Script

## 30-Second Pitch

ReliefRoute is a real-time emergency resource heatmap that helps people find nearby shelters, water, power, Wi-Fi, clinics, and cooling centers during emergencies. During a power outage or heat wave, information is usually scattered and outdated. Our app connects people to the best nearby resource using location, availability, trust score, and live community reports.

## Demo Scenario

A power outage happens in Los Angeles.

The user needs:

- Phone charging
- Wi-Fi
- A safe nearby place

## Demo Steps

1. Open landing page.
2. Click "Find Help Now."
3. Choose emergency type: Power Outage.
4. Choose needs: Power and Wi-Fi.
5. Show best recommended resource.
6. Open the map and resource markers.
7. Click the best resource.
8. Show trust score and status.
9. Submit a new report: "Wi-Fi working, charging available."
10. Show that the resource was updated.

## Judge Closing Line

ReliefRoute turns emergency information into a live network of trusted resources, helping people quickly find the safest and most useful place to go.
